About Splinters

Splinters is a social group of amature to semi-pro mountainboarders who meet to practice, participate and enjoy mountainboarding.

We come in all shapes and sizes and from all walks of life. but the one thing we all enjoy is the rush of speeding down a hill using nothing but balance to control your direction

Some of our more experienced boarders also enjoy using parachutes to speed across flat ground.
